Green house gass monitoring system

The IoT-Based Greenhouse Gas Monitoring System leverages the ESP32 microcontroller to analyze greenhouse gas concentrations in real-time. It measures gases like CO2, CH4, and N2O, providing critical data to authorized personnel in factories and greenhouse-releasing industries. This system enhances compliance with environmental regulations and supports sustainability efforts by delivering timely alerts and insights, empowering industries to take proactive measures in reducing their carbon footprint.
